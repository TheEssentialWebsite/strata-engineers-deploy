Paul G. Carter, P.E., G.E. - Your Foundation Evaluation Expert

Professional Background

Paul G. Carter brings over 35 years of comprehensive geotechnical engineering experience to Strata Consulting Engineers. As a licensed Professional Engineer in Utah, Nevada, and California, and a certified Geotechnical Engineer in California, Paul has dedicated his career to understanding the complex relationships between soil conditions, foundation systems, and structural performance.

Education & Credentials

  • Bachelor of Science in Civil Engineering, Brigham Young University
  • Master of Science in Civil Engineering, Brigham Young University
  • Licensed Professional Engineer (P.E.) - Utah, Nevada, California
  • Geotechnical Engineer (G.E.) - California
  • Published researcher in liquefaction risk assessment
  • 35+ years of geotechnical engineering experience

Areas of Expertise

Foundation Evaluation & Assessment

Paul specializes in comprehensive foundation evaluations for residential and light commercial properties. His assessments include detailed visual inspections, settlement analysis, crack pattern evaluation, and practical recommendations for foundation maintenance and repair.

Soil Condition Analysis

With extensive experience in Southern Utah's unique geological conditions, Paul provides expert analysis of soil properties, bearing capacity, settlement potential, and the impact of local environmental factors on foundation performance.

Geotechnical Consulting

Paul offers strategic geotechnical guidance for property owners, developers, and contractors, helping them navigate soil challenges, foundation design considerations, and regulatory requirements with confidence.

Non-Destructive Testing

Utilizing advanced NDT equipment and methodologies, Paul evaluates existing concrete structures to assess integrity, strength, and durability without causing damage to the structure.

Professional Philosophy

Paul's approach to geotechnical consulting is rooted in practical problem-solving and clear communication. He believes that every foundation challenge has a solution, and his role is to provide property owners with the information and guidance they need to make informed decisions about their investments.

Local Expertise

Having worked extensively throughout Southern Utah, Paul has developed deep insights into the region's unique geological characteristics, including expansive soils, seasonal moisture variations, and seismic considerations. This local knowledge enables him to provide targeted recommendations that address the specific challenges faced by property owners in the area.

Professional Commitment

Paul is committed to staying current with industry best practices, technological advances, and regulatory changes. His ongoing professional development ensures that Strata Consulting Engineers' clients receive the most up-to-date and effective guidance for their foundation evaluation and geotechnical consulting needs.
