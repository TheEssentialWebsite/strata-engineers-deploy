Non-Destructive Testing (NDT) Services

Strata Consulting Engineers provides comprehensive non-destructive testing services for concrete structures and foundation systems. Our advanced NDT capabilities allow us to evaluate structural integrity, material properties, and construction quality without causing damage to existing structures.

Why Choose Non-Destructive Testing?

Non-destructive testing offers numerous advantages for foundation evaluation and structural assessment:

  • Preserve structural integrity during evaluation
  • Cost-effective assessment of existing conditions
  • Rapid data collection and analysis
  • Minimal disruption to building operations
  • Comprehensive evaluation of large areas
  • Objective, quantitative results

Our NDT Services

Ultrasonic Pulse Velocity (UPV) Testing

Advanced ultrasonic testing technology provides detailed information about concrete quality, uniformity, and structural integrity.

Applications:

  • Concrete quality assessment
  • Void and crack detection
  • Uniformity evaluation
  • Elastic modulus estimation
  • Deterioration monitoring

Rebound Hammer Testing

Surface hardness testing provides rapid assessment of concrete strength and identifies areas requiring further investigation.

Capabilities:

  • Compressive strength estimation
  • Surface hardness mapping
  • Deterioration identification
  • Quality control verification
  • Comparative analysis between areas

Reinforcement Locating

Advanced electromagnetic technology accurately locates and maps steel reinforcement within concrete structures.

Services Include:

  • Rebar location and depth measurement
  • Reinforcement pattern mapping
  • Cover thickness determination
  • Pre-drilling investigation
  • Renovation planning support

NDT Applications

Foundation Evaluation

Comprehensive assessment of foundation concrete quality, identifying potential problems before they compromise structural performance.

Structural Assessment

Evaluation of existing concrete structures to determine current condition and remaining service life.

Quality Control

Verification of construction quality and compliance with design specifications for new construction projects.

Renovation Support

Pre-construction investigation to support renovation and modification projects while protecting existing structural elements.
