Areas of Expertise - Foundation Evaluation & Geotechnical Services

Strata Consulting Engineers specializes in comprehensive foundation evaluation and geotechnical consulting services. Our expertise spans residential and light commercial applications, with a focus on practical, cost-effective solutions for property owners throughout Southern Utah and beyond.

Foundation Assessment & Evaluation

Comprehensive Foundation Inspection Services

Our foundation assessment services provide detailed evaluations of existing residential and commercial foundation systems. We specialize in identifying settlement issues, structural concerns, and potential problems before they become costly repairs.

Visual Foundation Inspection

Thorough visual examination of foundation walls, footings, and related structural elements to identify cracks, settlement, moisture intrusion, and other signs of distress.

Settlement Analysis

Professional assessment of foundation movement patterns, including differential settlement evaluation and recommendations for monitoring and mitigation.

Crack Pattern Evaluation

Expert analysis of foundation crack patterns to determine causes, assess severity, and recommend appropriate repair strategies.

Geotechnical Consulting

Expert Soil Condition Analysis & Recommendations

Our geotechnical consulting services help property owners understand how soil conditions impact foundation performance and structural stability.

Soil Condition Review

Comprehensive analysis of existing geotechnical reports and soil data to identify potential foundation risks and development challenges.

Site-Specific Recommendations

Customized guidance based on local soil conditions, climate factors, and project requirements for optimal foundation performance.

Regulatory Compliance Support

Assistance with local building code requirements and regulatory approval processes for foundation-related projects.

Concrete Non-Destructive Testing (NDT)

Advanced Testing Without Structural Damage

Our NDT services utilize state-of-the-art equipment to evaluate concrete integrity, strength, and condition without causing damage to existing structures.

Ultrasonic Pulse Velocity (UPV) Testing

Advanced ultrasonic testing to assess concrete quality, detect voids, and evaluate structural integrity throughout the concrete matrix.

Rebound Hammer Analysis

Surface hardness testing to estimate concrete strength and identify areas of potential concern or deterioration.

Reinforcement Location

Non-invasive detection and mapping of steel reinforcement within concrete structures to support renovation and modification projects.

Foundation Repair Consulting
