Our Consultation Process

At Strata Consulting Engineers, we've developed a streamlined consultation process designed to provide you with expert foundation evaluation and geotechnical guidance efficiently and effectively.

Step 1: Schedule Your Consultation

Getting Started is Easy

Use our online contact form or call us directly at 435-216-1221 to schedule your initial consultation. We offer both virtual and on-site consultation options to meet your specific needs and preferences.

Consultation Options:

  • Virtual Consultation: Convenient remote discussion for project planning and initial assessment
  • On-Site Consultation: In-person evaluation for detailed foundation assessment and site-specific analysis
  • Document Review: Professional evaluation of existing reports and documentation

Step 2: Discuss Your Project

Understanding Your Needs

During your consultation, we'll discuss your project goals, review any existing documentation, and assess site conditions. This collaborative approach ensures we understand your specific situation and can provide targeted recommendations.

What We'll Cover:

  • Project objectives and timeline
  • Current foundation concerns or observations
  • Site history and previous construction
  • Budget considerations and priorities
  • Regulatory requirements and permitting needs

Step 3: Receive Expert Guidance

Actionable Recommendations

Based on our assessment, you'll receive clear, practical recommendations tailored to your specific situation. Our guidance includes next steps, contractor referrals when appropriate, and professional documentation as needed.

Deliverables May Include:

  • Written summary of findings and recommendations
  • Photographic documentation of site conditions
  • Referrals to qualified contractors and specialists
  • Guidance on additional testing or evaluation needs
  • Timeline and budget considerations for recommended actions

Consultation Pricing

Our consultation fees are structured to provide excellent value while ensuring comprehensive professional service:

  • Virtual Consultations: Starting at competitive hourly rates
  • On-Site Consultations: Flat fee structure based on scope and location
  • Travel Considerations: Additional fees may apply for locations outside our primary service area
  • Follow-Up Services: Discounted rates for ongoing project support

What to Expect

Professional Expertise

Every consultation is conducted by Paul G. Carter, P.E., G.E., bringing over 35 years of geotechnical engineering experience to your project.

Timely Response

We understand that foundation concerns can be urgent. We strive to respond to consultation requests promptly and accommodate urgent situations when possible.

Clear Communication

Our consultation process emphasizes clear, jargon-free communication. You'll receive practical recommendations you can understand and act upon.

Ongoing Support

Our relationship doesn't end with the initial consultation. We're available for follow-up questions and ongoing project support as your needs evolve.

Preparing for Your Consultation

To maximize the value of your consultation, consider preparing:

  • Recent photographs of foundation areas and any visible concerns
  • Existing engineering reports, soil studies, or construction documents
  • Property history, including known foundation issues or repairs
  • List of specific questions or concerns
  • Project timeline and budget considerations
