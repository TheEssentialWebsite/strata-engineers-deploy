Comprehensive Foundation Evaluation & Geotechnical Services

At Strata Consulting Engineers, we provide specialized foundation evaluation and geotechnical consulting services designed to help property owners, developers, and contractors make informed decisions about their projects. Our comprehensive approach combines decades of experience with practical, cost-effective solutions.

Foundation Evaluations

Comprehensive visual assessment of existing residential and commercial foundations

Our detailed foundation evaluations identify settlement issues, foundation concerns, and recommend cost-effective solutions. Each evaluation includes visual inspection, measurement of movement, crack pattern analysis, and customized recommendations for existing foundations.

Geotechnical Consultations

Expert analysis of soil conditions and their impact on existing structures

We provide comprehensive soil condition reviews and interpretations of geotechnical reports to identify potential foundation risks. Our analysis helps property owners and developers understand how local soil conditions affect foundation stability and longevity.

Project Reviews

Professional evaluation of engineering reports and project documentation

Our expert review services analyze existing engineering documentation, foundation reports, and project specifications to validate findings, identify concerns, and ensure regulatory compliance before proceeding with your project.

On-Site Consultations

Direct field assessment and expert guidance

Paul G. Carter provides in-person evaluations of foundations, existing structures, and construction sites to identify issues and provide immediate professional insights. These consultations include thorough documentation and practical next steps.

Concrete NDT Assessment

Non-destructive testing for existing concrete integrity and strength

Using advanced equipment including Ultrasonic Pulse Velocity (UPV) testing, rebound hammer analysis, and reinforcement locators, we evaluate existing concrete structures without causing damage. These assessments provide critical data on foundation integrity and durability.

Foundation Repair Consultations

Independent evaluation of repair options and contractor proposals

Our unbiased assessments help property owners understand repair proposals, evaluate contractor recommendations, and select the most appropriate and cost-effective foundation repair solutions for their specific existing foundation situation.

Expert Witness Services

Professional testimony and consultation for construction-related disputes

Drawing on decades of geotechnical engineering experience, Paul G. Carter provides expert witness services for legal proceedings related to foundation issues, construction defects, and other geotechnical engineering matters with clear, authoritative testimony and documentation.

Educational Resources

Beyond our direct consulting services, Strata Consulting Engineers is committed to educating property owners and industry professionals about foundation evaluation best practices, soil condition awareness, and proactive foundation maintenance strategies.
