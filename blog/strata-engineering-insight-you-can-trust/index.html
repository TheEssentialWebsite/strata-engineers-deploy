Who Is Strata Consulting Engineers? Meet the Expertise Behind the Name

Strata Consulting Engineers isn’t your typical engineering firm. Led by Paul G. Carter, Professional Engineer (Civil and Geotechnical), Strata combines decades of deep technical expertise with hands-on experience in the field. When Paul walks onto a job site or reviews a report, he brings both perspectives to the table: that of a seasoned engineer and a contractor who knows how things actually get built, rebuilt, or added onto.

Foundation Assessment & Evaluation

Foundation issues can be scary, especially when you’re buying or selling a property. Strata provides unbiased, engineering-driven assessments that help clarify what’s really going on beneath your feet. Whether it's structural cracks, settlement, or water intrusion, Paul and the team identify root causes and recommend appropriate solutions without pushing unnecessary repairs. With experience overseeing thousands of repair projects, Paul knows how to distinguish between superficial signs of age and serious structural problems. His assessments often save clients from spending thousands on unnecessary work or alert them early to issues that could develop into costly repairs. Geotechnical Consulting

Soil matters more than most people realize. Paul’s background includes leading geotechnical investigations on hundreds of residential and commercial sites, performing percolation testing for septic systems, analyzing slope stability for hillside developments, and preparing recommendations to mitigate liquefaction risks in seismically active zones. His work with Mid-Coast Geotechnical in California gave him hands-on experience managing teams, overseeing lab and field testing, and compiling results into practical, code-compliant recommendations. Whether it's a single-family home or commercial, new construction, repair, or expansion, Paul can incorporate his experience to help you understand your geotechnical/foundation engineering reports before you proceed.

Concrete Non-Destructive Testing (NDT)

Sometimes you need to know what’s inside the concrete without tearing it apart. That’s where NDT comes in. Using industry-leading tools and methods, Strata can evaluate the strength, consistency, and embedded features of existing concrete structures. This is crucial in old or new concrete, for retrofits, additions, or inspections to avoid damaging the existing concrete. Paul’s NDT expertise isn’t just academic; he ran SDS Non-Destructive Testing Equipment, a company that sold, trained, and demoed NDT tools across the country. That hands-on knowledge means he knows the difference between a clean scan and a misleading one. He understands the limitations and strengths of each tool, ensuring that his interpretations lead to clear, confident decisions. Strata’s NDT services are especially useful for engineers, contractors, and inspectors who don’t have access to NDT equipment. Explore our NDT services
